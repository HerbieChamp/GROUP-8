About

Kalinga is both a land of legend and mystery. Kalinga is currently making waves in the world of ecotourism. Mystic seekers, hikers/Mountaineers, adventures, cultural advocates, and nature lovers are increasingly flocking to Kalinga. The Journey to Kalinga province is an adventure in and of itself. if you're not a fan of long bumpy rides, you might want to reconsider this one. However, if you enjoy nature and adventure, the Kalinga Province should be on your must-visit list. Kalinga is now designated as a Tourism Development Area. The Kalinga Peoples, who live in a beautiful are of rice terraces and white water rapids on the Chico River, are known for their strong tribal awareness and were never subdued by Spanish colonizers during their more than 300-year time in charge in the Philippines. Kalinga is a place for adventures but also for ones who need time away from the hustle and bustle of the city life
