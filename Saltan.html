Saltan River

Saltan river comes from the vast mossy forests of Balbalan. During good sunny conditions, the waters exude an emerald appearance, making it inviting for a swim. Cavernous boulders and rocks surround the river. These provide a perfect spot for diving or jumping. The river teems with endemic aquatic species such as fishes and eels. More often than not, there are being served to visitors the traditional way. Because of its pristine condition, Saltan River is one of the frequented tourist spots in Kalinga especially among the locals.

  • Location: Balbalan, Kalinga
  • Trekking time: 20-30 Minutes
  • Guide fee: Donation
  • Entrance: None
