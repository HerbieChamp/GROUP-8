Balbalasang-Balbalan National Park

The park represents the ecological bounty of Kalinga province and even the whole country. Mossy forests cover most of the park along with patches of pine and montane forests. So far, 89 species of birds, 23 species of mammals, 13 species of amphibians, 13 species of reptiles and 25 species of earthworms have been documented in the area. The park also provides a haven to the critically-endangered Rafflesia. Exploring this park would lead you into a rustic and thrilling adventure. Who knows? You might catch sight of the aforesaid wildlife species. Indeed, this will definitely rise as one of the top tourist spots in Kalinga.

  • Location: Balbalan, Kalinga
  • Trekking time: 2-3 Days
  • Guide fee: Donation
  • Entrance: None
