Pasil Natural Dam

Pasil Natural Dam lies just below the community of barangay Dangtalan, specifically, sitio Puapo. A massive slope erosion formed this dam-like basin of water. It offers a good spot for boating and picnic during the summer months. Swimming, however, is discouraged due to possible water contamination because of small-scale mining operations upstream. When one follows the river downstream, there is the towering and multi-tier Laroy Falls.

  • Location: Pasil, kalinga
  • Trekking time: 20-30 Minutes
  • Guide fee: Donation
  • Entrance: None
