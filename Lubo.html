Lubo & Mangali Rice Terraces

From the road, these terraces look like circular cascades. They surround the village of Lubo. The Tanudan river passes alongside these terraces. On a personal note, these are among the most picturesque tourist spots in Kalinga. Towering mountains provide a dramatic background of these terraces. They also supply abundant water supply all year round. Aside from the terraces, the hospitality of the people would definitely leave you feeling blissful.

  • Location: Tanudan, Kalinga
  • Trekking time: Minutes away from the Village
  • Guide fee: Donation
  • Entrance: None
